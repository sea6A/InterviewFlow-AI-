# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful and collaborative.

Examples of positive behavior:

- giving constructive technical feedback
- assuming good intent
- discussing ideas without personal attacks
- helping improve clarity in code, docs, and reviews

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or insulting language
- publishing private information
- hostile or disruptive conduct in issues or pull requests

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this standard.
